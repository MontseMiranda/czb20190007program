Hotel Urban Gran Ducat

Urban Gran Ducat is situated next to Passeig de Gracia Boulevard, 600 m from Gaudi’s famous Casa Batlló and a 5-minute walk from the Ramblas and the Gothic Quarter. Metro and regional train services stop at Catalunya Station, just 50 m away.

Set in a restored 19th- century building, the Gran Ducat Hotel features simple, modern décor throughout.

A varied buffet breakfast is available in the Gran Ducat’s dining room. You can also find a wide range of cafés, tapas bars and restaurants in the surrounding streets.
