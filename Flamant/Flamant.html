Restaurant Flamant

Flamant is a large restaurant with capacity for more than 200 people that presents a quite old-fashioned space with restored furniture and natural sunlight.

Elegant, noble and sophisticated, Flamant is located on the popular, centrally located Carrer Enrique Granados. Its large rooms, meticulously designed, give diners an exclusive experience in a pleasant setting.

The food is traditional and innovative at once, feature the best of Mediterranean cuisine.

Location: Eixample left

Dining room capacities: 220 pax

Price Level: average-medium

Style: Mediterranean and market cuisine

Vegetarian Options/Special diets: Available

Handicapped facilities: Yes
