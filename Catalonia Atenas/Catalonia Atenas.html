Hotel Catalonia Atenas

Set 3 metro stops from central Barcelona, the Atenas Catalonia has a rooftop swimming pool, offering views of the nearby Torre Agbar and Sagrada Familia. It features a bright rooms of Hotel Atenas Barcelona and a simple décor with wooden flooring. In the vicinity of the Catalonia Atenas and very close by on foot you can visit the Sagrada Familia and the modernist Recinte Sant Pau (formerly a hospital). The Les Glòries shopping centre is a 15-minute walk away.

The hotel's buffet breakfast features a show kitchen. There is a snack bar and guests can enjoy stunning city views from the pool, set on the 11th floor.
